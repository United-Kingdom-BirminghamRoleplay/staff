ERLC Punishment Guidelines

Comprehensive FailRP enforcement and punishment procedures for Emergency Response: Liberty County

Core Enforcement Principles

  • Roleplay First: Always prioritize immersive ERLC experience
  • Evidence Required: Screenshot/record violations for bans only (not required for warnings/kicks)
  • Fair & Consistent: Apply punishments equally regardless of player status
  • Escalation Protocol: Complex cases go to Senior Staff
  • Documentation: Log ALL punishments in external system immediately
  • Session Reset: All warnings reset each session - no carryover

UKBRUM In-Game Offences & Actions

  • Interrupting Scenes: Warning + Load/Refresh

Instant Ban Offences (No Side Actions)

  • Breaking ToS: Instant Ban
  • Major Events: Instant Ban
  • L-Tap/NITRP: Instant Ban
  • Raiding: Instant Ban

Important Notes

Session Reset Policy

All warnings are reset each session. Old warnings from earlier sessions do not carry over to new sessions.

Moderator Discretion

Kick durations are decided by the moderator handling the case, depending on the severity of the offence.
