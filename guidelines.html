ERLC Punishment Guidelines

Comprehensive FailRP enforcement and punishment procedures for Emergency Response: Liberty County

⚖️ Core Enforcement Principles

  • 🎭 Roleplay First: Always prioritize immersive ERLC experience
  • 📸 Evidence Required: Screenshot/record all violations before action
  • ⚖️ Fair & Consistent: Apply punishments equally regardless of player status
  • 📈 Escalation Protocol: Complex FailRP cases go to Senior Staff
  • 📋 Documentation: Log ALL punishments in external system immediately

🚨 ERLC Punishment Levels

Level 1 - Minor FailRP 🟡

Violations: Minor character breaks, mild unrealistic behavior, first-time rule confusion

Examples: Wrong radio codes, minor vehicle misuse, character name issues

Actions: Verbal warning, character correction guidance

Authority: All staff members (Trainee+)

Level 2 - Moderate FailRP 🟠

Violations: Repeated minor FailRP, moderate character breaks, inappropriate vehicle use

Examples: Civilian using emergency vehicles, unrealistic driving, minor RDM

Actions: Server kick, 30min-2hr temporary ban

Authority: Trial Moderator and above

Level 3 - Serious FailRP 🔴

Violations: Major character breaks, RDM/VDM, trolling, department impersonation

Examples: Random killing, intentional crashes, fake LEO/EMS, exploiting

Actions: 1-7 day ban, department blacklist

Authority: Moderator and above

Level 4 - Severe FailRP ⚫

Violations: Extreme trolling, mass RDM/VDM, server disruption, harassment

Examples: Spawn killing, mass vehicle ramming, targeted harassment, doxxing threats

Actions: 1-4 week ban, permanent department blacklist

Authority: Administrator and above

Level 5 - Critical Violations 🚫

Violations: Exploiting, hacking, real-world threats, ban evasion, NSFW content

Examples: Speed hacking, money exploits, doxxing, alt account usage, inappropriate content

Actions: Permanent ban, IP ban, Discord ban

Authority: Senior Administrator and above

🏥 Department-Specific Guidelines

🚔 Law Enforcement FailRP

  • Corruption without permission: Immediate department removal + 3 day ban
  • Unrealistic arrests: Warning → Department kick → Blacklist
  • Equipment misuse: Equipment removal → Department suspension
  • Chain of command violations: Verbal warning → Written warning → Demotion

🚑 Emergency Medical Services

  • Refusing medical aid: Warning → Department kick
  • Unrealistic medical RP: Guidance → Retraining → Removal
  • Vehicle misuse: Vehicle removal → Department suspension

🔥 Fire & Rescue

  • Ignoring emergency calls: Warning → Department kick
  • Equipment trolling: Immediate removal + server punishment
  • Unrealistic operations: Retraining → Department probation

📋 Appeal Process

  1. 🎫 Submit Appeal: Player uses designated Discord appeal channel
  2. 👀 Initial Review: Issuing staff member reviews within 24 hours
  3. ⚖️ Evidence Assessment: All evidence and context reviewed
  4. 🔄 Senior Review: If upheld, appeal escalates to Senior Staff
  5. ✅ Final Decision: Decision communicated within 48 hours maximum
  6. 📝 Documentation: All appeal outcomes logged in system

📸 Evidence & Documentation Standards

Required for ALL Punishments

  • 📷 Visual Evidence: Screenshots or video of violation
  • 🕐 Timestamp: Exact time and date of incident
  • 👤 Player Info: Roblox username, display name, User ID
  • 📍 Location: Where in ERLC the violation occurred
  • 📝 Description: Detailed explanation of what happened
  • 👮 Staff Member: Who issued the punishment
  • ⚖️ Rule Violated: Specific ERLC rule reference
