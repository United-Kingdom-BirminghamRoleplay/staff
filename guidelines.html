Punishment Guidelines

Punishment guidelines exist to ensure fairness, consistency, and transparency when dealing with rule-breaking in Birmingham Roleplay. They provide staff with a structured framework for determining the appropriate response to different types of misconduct, helping to prevent punishments from being either too harsh or too lenient.

Important Notes

Session Reset Policy

All warnings are reset each session. Old warnings from earlier sessions do not carry over to new sessions.

Moderator Discretion

Kick durations are decided by the moderator handling the case, depending on the severity of the offence.

📋 Appeal Process

  1. 🎫 Submit Appeal: Player uses designated Discord appeal channel
  2. 👀 Initial Review: Issuing staff member reviews within 24 hours
  3. ⚖️ Evidence Assessment: All evidence and context reviewed
  4. 🔄 Senior Review: If upheld, appeal escalates to Senior Staff
  5. ✅ Final Decision: Decision communicated within 48 hours maximum
  6. 📝 Documentation: All appeal outcomes logged in system

📸 Evidence & Documentation Standards

Required for ALL Punishments

  • 📷 Visual Evidence: Screenshots or video of violation
  • 🕐 Timestamp: Exact time and date of incident
  • 👤 Player Info: Roblox username, display name, User ID
  • 📍 Location: Where in ERLC the violation occurred
  • 📝 Description: Detailed explanation of what happened
  • 👮 Staff Member: Who issued the punishment
  • ⚖️ Rule Violated: Specific ERLC rule reference
