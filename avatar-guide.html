Roblox Avatar Guide

In your role as a staff member at Birmingham Roleplay, presenting a professional and appropriate Roblox avatar is not just a suggestion, but a pivotal requirement, as it directly impacts how seriously you're taken and ensures you're always prepared to uphold our community's standards effectively and respectfully.

Your avatar mainly should appear professional. It should have nothing blocking the uniform to a large extent.
