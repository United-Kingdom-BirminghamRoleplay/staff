Punishment Guide

Punishment guidelines exist to ensure fairness, consistency, and transparency when dealing with rule-breaking in Birmingham Roleplay. They provide staff with a structured framework for determining the appropriate response to different types of misconduct, helping to prevent punishments from being either too harsh or too lenient.

Multiple warning offenses together can add up to cause that user to be kicked. For example: Staff Disrespect + Cuff Rushing + Failing to Roleplay could mean that person gets kicked.
