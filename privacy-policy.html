Privacy Policy

How we handle your data on the UKBRUM Staff Portal

Data Collection & Usage

Authentication Data

We collect your staff rank and login credentials to provide access to the portal. Login attempts are logged with timestamps and IP addresses for security purposes.

Form Data

When you create or submit forms, we store the form content, responses, and associated metadata. This includes your staff rank as the creator and submission timestamps.

Report Submissions

General reports collect your Roblox username, Discord username, report type, description, and optional evidence links for staff review purposes.

Announcements

Staff announcements include the creator's rank, creation date, and content for organizational communication purposes.

Data Storage & Security

Server Storage

All data is stored on secure servers in JSON format with automatic backups. Access to data files is restricted through server-side security measures.

Backup System

We maintain automatic backups of all data with a retention policy of 10 most recent backups to ensure data integrity and recovery capabilities.

Access Control

Data access is restricted based on staff rank. Senior Management has additional privileges for form PIN access and announcement creation.

Data Sharing & Notifications

Discord Integration

Form submissions, reports, and announcements trigger notifications to designated Discord channels for staff coordination. No personal data beyond what you submit is shared.

Internal Use Only

All collected data is used exclusively for UKBRUM staff operations and is not shared with external parties or used for commercial purposes.

Your Rights

Data Access

You can view your submitted forms and responses through the portal. Senior Management can access additional data as required for their role.

Data Removal

Contact Senior Management if you need specific data removed from the system. We will process reasonable requests in accordance with operational requirements.

Questions & Concerns

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, contact Senior Management through Discord or the staff portal.

Last Updated

This privacy policy was last updated on October 9, 2025. We may update this policy as needed to reflect changes in our data handling practices.
