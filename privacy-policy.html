Privacy Policy

How we handle your data on the UKBRUM Staff Portal

Data Collection & Usage

Authentication Data

We collect your Roblox username, Discord username, staff rank, and encrypted passwords to provide access to the portal. Login attempts, security events, and IP addresses are logged with timestamps for security monitoring.

Form Data & Assessments

When you create or submit forms and assessments, we store the content, responses, grades, and metadata in our MySQL database. This includes creator information, submission timestamps, and assessment results with Discord webhook notifications.

File Uploads & Reports

File uploads, trial logs, and reports collect your username, content, timestamps, and approval status. All submissions are stored securely with access controls based on staff rank hierarchy.

Announcements

Staff announcements include the creator's rank, creation date, and content for organizational communication purposes.

Data Storage & Security

Database Storage

All data is stored in a secure MySQL database with proper indexing and relationships. Database access is restricted through authentication and server-side security measures with regular backups.

Security Monitoring

We maintain comprehensive security logs including IP banning system, login monitoring, and automated threat detection with Discord webhook notifications for critical security events.

Rank-Based Access Control

Data access follows a 9-tier rank hierarchy from Moderation (1) to Founder (9). Higher ranks have additional privileges for user management, assessment creation, and administrative functions.

Data Sharing & Notifications

Discord Integration

Form submissions, reports, and announcements trigger notifications to designated Discord channels for staff coordination. No personal data beyond what you submit is shared.

Internal Use Only

All collected data is used exclusively for UKBRUM staff operations and is not shared with external parties or used for commercial purposes.

Your Rights

Data Access

You can view your submitted forms and responses through the portal. Senior Management can access additional data as required for their role.

Data Removal

Contact Senior Management if you need specific data removed from the system. We will process reasonable requests in accordance with operational requirements.

Questions & Concerns

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, contact Senior Management through Discord or the staff portal.

Last Updated

This privacy policy was last updated on the19th of November 2025. We may update this policy as needed to reflect changes in our data handling practices.
