ERLC Staff Training Program

New Staff Onboarding Checklist

  • ERLC Rules Mastery: Complete understanding of all FailRP rules
  • Department Knowledge: Learn LEO, EMS, Fire department procedures
  • Command Training: Practice all admin commands in test environment
  • Shadow Period: 5 shifts with experienced staff member
  • Scenario Training: Handle mock FailRP situations
  • Logging System: Master external punishment logging
  • Discord Integration: Join all staff channels and learn protocols
  • Final Assessment: Pass comprehensive ERLC knowledge test

Ongoing Staff Development

  • Weekly Briefings: Mandatory Sunday staff meetings
  • ERLC Updates: Training on new game features and rules
  • Scenario Drills: Monthly FailRP scenario practice
  • Peer Reviews: Quarterly performance evaluations
  • Advanced Certifications: Specialized department training
  • Performance Metrics: Monthly activity and quality reviews
