Staff Policies

Social Media Policy

Sharing personal social media accounts with staff, community members, or external parties is strictly prohibited to protect privacy and maintain professional boundaries.

Staff Conduct Policy

Our community maintains a safe, respectful environment free from harassment. All unwelcome behavior creating hostile environments is strictly prohibited.

Advertisement Policy

Staff are prohibited from advertising or promoting other communities. Focus must remain on supporting Birmingham Roleplay without using position for personal gain.

Sharepoint Confidentiality

Staff Sharepoint content is confidential. Sharing, distributing, or leaking any information to unauthorized individuals is strictly prohibited.

Professionalism Policy

All staff must demonstrate professionalism at all times, treating players and colleagues with respect, fairness, and maturity in all situations.

Anti-Raiding Policy

Using commands to troll, harass, or disrupt the community is command abuse. Powers must be used responsibly for community benefit only.
