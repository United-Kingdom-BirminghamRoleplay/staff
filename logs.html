Melonly Logging Requirements

Mandatory Information for ALL Logs

  • Player Details: Roblox username, display name, User ID
  • Violation Type: Specific UKBRUM rule violation with category
  • Evidence: Screenshots/video for bans (optional for warnings/kicks)
  • Punishment: Type, duration, and severity level
  • Staff Member: Who issued the punishment (with rank)
  • Timestamp: Exact date/time of incident and punishment
  • Location: Where in ERLC the violation occurred
  • Department: If applicable (LEO, EMS, Fire, Civilian)

🗂️ ERLC Log Categories

Category Description Required Evidence
🎭 FailRP Violations Character breaks, unrealistic behavior, rule confusion Screenshots of behavior, chat logs, context
💀 RDM/VDM Incidents Random killing, vehicle ramming, intentional crashes Video evidence, death logs, witness statements
👮 Department FailRP LEO/EMS/Fire violations, corruption, equipment misuse Department activity logs, screenshots, supervisor reports
🔧 Exploiting/Hacking Speed hacks, money exploits, game manipulation Video proof, server logs, technical evidence
🎯 Trolling/Griefing Intentional disruption, mass chaos, server trolling Multiple screenshots/videos, impact assessment
🔄 Ban Evasion Alt accounts, IP circumvention, return after ban Account linking evidence, IP logs, behavior patterns
📋 Appeals Player appeals and their outcomes Original punishment log, appeal reasoning, decision

⏰ Staff Logging Responsibilities

ALL UKBRUM Staff Must:

  • 📝 Immediate Logging: Log ALL punishments within 15 minutes of issuing
  • 📸 Evidence First: Gather evidence BEFORE taking action
  • 🎯 Accurate Categories: Use correct ERLC violation categories
  • 🔄 Update Logs: Modify entries if punishments are appealed/changed
  • 🔒 Confidentiality: Maintain privacy of sensitive player information
  • 📊 Weekly Reports: Submit activity summaries to Senior Staff
  • 🎓 Training Logs: Document all staff training and certifications

🚨 Priority Logging Situations

Immediate Senior Staff Notification Required:

  • 🔧 Exploiting/Hacking: Any suspected game manipulation
  • 👥 Mass Incidents: Multiple players involved in major FailRP
  • 🎯 Targeted Harassment: Personal attacks or doxxing attempts
  • 🏢 Department Corruption: Unauthorized LEO/EMS corruption RP
  • 🔄 Ban Evasion: Confirmed alt account usage
  • ⚖️ Staff Misconduct: Any staff member rule violations
