Session Team

One of the additional assignments you can take on as a High Ranking Staff member is joining the Session Team. This team plays an important role in helping run and manage sessions smoothly. A weekly roster will be created to assign responsibilities and ensure everything is organized. If you are unable to attend your scheduled session, you must contact your Segment Lead as soon as possible so adjustments can be made. This helps keep sessions running efficiently and ensures there is always proper coverage.
