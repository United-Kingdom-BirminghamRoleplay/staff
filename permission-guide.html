Permission Guide

Permission Guide explains what permissions access each rank provides. It shows you which rank you give And areas players can use depending on the rank you assign them. This helps make sure everyone has the right level of access for their role in the game, and reduces significant load from each staff.

Permission Types

Permission Type Rank Explaination
Gun Permissions Senior Moderator+ Gun permissions can only be granted by Senior Moderator+. When you proceed to give gun permissions out you need to ensure that there are a minimum amount of people who currently have gun permissions, to grant the next person gun permissions. You need to explain to the user to use them appropriately otherwise they will face moderation action.
Hostage Permissions Trial Administrator+ Hostage permissions can only be granted by Trial Administrator+. When you proceed to give hostage permissions out you need to ensure that there are a minimum amount of people who currently have gun permissions, to grant the next person hostage permissions. You need to explain to the user to follow Roblox terms of service and roleplay accordingly otherwise they will face moderation action.
