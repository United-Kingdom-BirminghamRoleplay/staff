Melonly Systems

Melonly is our moderation system designed to log and record staff activity. It helps track actions taken by staff members. This system is useful for monitoring in-game activity helping our high ranking team identify what rule is most frequently broken in-game and records players logs for each rule they break.

Melonly Guidelines

  • Accuracy: Ensure all logs are accurate and detailed. Include relevant information such as player names, IDs, timestamps, and a clear description of the incident.
  • Timeliness: Submit logs as soon as possible after the incident occurs to ensure the information is fresh and reliable.
  • Professionalism: Maintain a professional tone in all logs. Avoid using slang or informal language.
  • Confidentiality: Do not share sensitive information from logs with unauthorized personnel. Respect player privacy and data protection regulations.
  • Consistency: Follow the established procedures for logging incidents to ensure consistency across all entries.
  • Review: Regularly review your logs for accuracy and completeness. Make corrections if necessary.
  • Training: Stay updated with any changes in the logging system or guidelines through regular training sessions.
